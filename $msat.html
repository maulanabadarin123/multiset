$MSAT

$MSAT is a native utility token of the Multisat ecosystem. It enables users to access exclusive Multisat features.

The core purposes of $MSAT token are to:
  1. Incentivize usage of the Multisat platform
  2. Encourage users to get involved in governance
  3. Enable a wide variety of platform privileges surrounding core technologies and ecosystem offerings
