FAQ

What is Multisat?

Multisat is a platform that connects BRC20 and EVM Network, allowing users to seamlessly transfer tokens between different blockchain ecosystems. It offers features such as wallet storage, token bridging, and a native token with governance rights and earning rewards for engaging with the platform.

How does multichain wallet work on Multisat?

Multisat developed multi-chain wallet that currently allow users to store, send and receive BRC20 Assets, Ethereum Assets and Solana Assets. More chain available soon.

What is Multisat dApps?

Multisat dApps offer token bridging service that transferring BRC20 tokens to the Ethereum network as ERC20 tokens or BNB, Solana. Users connect their wallets, select the desired tokens, and initiate the bridging process. Multibit ensures secure and efficient token migration, expanding the liquidity and opportunities available to users.More than that, Multisat dApps is also provide swap and purchase token services.

What are the advantage for holders?

$MSAT holders have access to tge Multisat DAO and Multisat Staking. Staking $MSAT tokens on Multisat allows users participate as nodes or provide liquidity, in order to earn rewards (in the form of ERC20 or BRC20 tokens) for their efforts. By staking their $MSAT tokens, participants contribute to the platform's liquidity and security, while passively earning additional token rewards based on their efforts in contributing to the network.
