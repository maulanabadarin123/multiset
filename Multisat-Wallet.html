Multisat Wallet

Multisat wallet is Multi-chain wallet supporting BTC, Ethereum and EVM-compatible chains in a unified multichain experience. Powered by the native Wallet Core SDK and governed by the Multisat community DAO through its own native token $MSAT.
Multisat’s vision for its wallet is a user-centric one, giving trustworthy access to the crypto world and the most popular wallet storage to users who also decide on the future of the platform through the decentralized governance.
Simple. Seamless. Enjoy a smooth mobile app and desktop experience with easy-to-use, powerful tools to support your entire Web3 journey. Deposit crypto easily from exchanges. Take control of your crypto. Avoid complicated steps and deposit directly to your wallet from exchanges like Binance and Coinbase. Stay private and secure. Rest easy knowing that our privacy and security measures keep you in control of your data and digital assets, while also keeping them safe.
