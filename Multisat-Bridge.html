How Does Multisat's Bridge Work?
The functionality of Multisatâ€™s Cross-Chain Bridge can be broken down into three key steps:
  1. Token Deposit and Lock: Users begin by depositing their BRC20 tokens into the Multisat Bridge. These tokens are securely locked within the bridge, ensuring a safe transition from the BRC20 network.
  2. Wrapped Token Issuance: Once the BRC20 tokens are deposited, users are issued equivalent wrapped tokens (such as ERC20) on the supported blockchain network. These wrapped tokens carry the same value as the original BRC20 tokens, allowing users to utilize them in different blockchain ecosystems.
  3. Redemption Mechanism: Multisat's bridge also provides a straightforward redemption process. Users can redeem their original BRC20 tokens anytime by burning the equivalent wrapped tokens, facilitating a smooth transition back to the BRC20 network.
